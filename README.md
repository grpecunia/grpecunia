### 👋 Hi, I'm [Gustavo Rivera]

**Co-Founder & CTO @ [Quibble](https://quibblerm.com/)** — science-based dynamic pricing & revenue management for short-term and vacation rentals (a.k.a. RevenueOS).

We built Quibble to close the gap between simple rules-based tools and true optimization. We pair the software with a full-service offering so operators get pricing peace of mind, not another dashboard to babysit. After years in travel and hospitality revenue management, we know the uncertainty the job brings — so we engineer and run custom pricing strategies and build partnerships that last.

> *"Sometimes attaining the deepest familiarity with a question is our best substitute for actually having the answer."* — B. Greene
